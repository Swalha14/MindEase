Understanding Mental Health

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how we think, feel, and act, and how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Why It Matters

Just like physical health, mental health is important at every stage of life. Poor mental health can affect daily functioning, relationships, and even physical well-being.

Signs of Mental Health Challenges

  • Prolonged sadness or irritability
  • Loss of interest in previously enjoyed activities
  • Changes in eating or sleeping habits
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Withdrawal from social interaction

How to Take Care of Your Mental Health

  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Exercise regularly
  • Maintain a healthy sleep routine
  • Practice mindfulness or relaxation techniques
  • Seek professional support when needed
